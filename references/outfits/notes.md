# Outfit Reference Notes

These notes summarize the outfit references provided for Hazel.

## Repeated Signals From The References

- strong use of black, charcoal, camel, cream, navy, and muted denim
- polished Korean styling rather than loud fashion styling
- mini skirt + boots works when the top half is strong and structured
- bomber jackets, cropped leather, wool coats, and padded outerwear all appear often
- fitted knit tops are preferred over cute blouses
- scarves, boots, and clean handbags help the looks feel expensive

## What To Borrow

- long camel or cream coat with jeans and scarf
- cropped leather jacket with fitted knit dress or skirt
- dark fitted knit top with plaid or straight skirt
- black/charcoal mini with tall boots and a sharp outer layer
- puffer + clean straight pants for casual winter days
- grey, navy, black, brown layering for school looks

## What To Adapt For Hazel

- many reference models are slimmer and flatter in the chest
- Hazel should not copy ultra-tight mini looks directly
- chest area needs cleaner fabrics, fewer details, and better structure
- Hazel will look better with:
  - open necklines
  - clear waist definition
  - stronger outerwear
  - slightly thicker fabrics

## Default Outfit Formulas

### School

- structured cardigan or neat bomber
- clean knit or open-collar base
- straight jeans or simple skirt
- tall boots or clean sneakers

### Weekend

- soft long coat or padded jacket
- fitted knit top
- high-waisted jeans or mini skirt with balance
- scarf or elevated bag

### Date

- fitted dress or fitted top + A-line mini
- leather or wool outer layer
- knee-high boots
- dark neutral palette with one soft contrast piece

## Buying Priorities

- knee-high black boots
- long camel coat
- dark brown leather jacket
- fitted charcoal knit dress
- high-waisted clean mini skirt
- straight-leg dark jeans
