- Age: 29
- Height: 163 cm
- Shape: proportionate overall, slightly fuller bust
- Sizes: top `M/L`, bottom `M/L`, shoes `EU 38`
- Main contexts: school, weekend outings, date looks

## Style Direction

Hazel's wardrobe should sit in this lane:

- Chinese-preferred polished femininity
- elegant and poised, not girlish
- refined with a little sensuality
- clean silhouettes with strong proportion control
- mature Korean-inspired urban styling

## Best Lines For Hazel

- soft V-neck
- square neck
- open collar shirts
- clean fitted knits
- waist-defined jackets
- high-waisted straight or slightly wide bottoms
- A-line mini skirts paired with strong outerwear
- long coats and tall boots to create vertical polish

## Avoid

- childish sweetness
- fussy details around the chest
- clingy thin fabrics over the bust
- shapeless oversized styling
- random trend stacking that looks cheap

## Styling Logic

- If the top is more fitted, keep the bottom clean and structured.
- If the skirt is shorter, add authority with a coat, blazer, or tall boots.
- If the bust line is more visible, avoid too many details and let the neckline stay clean.
- Keep "sexy" in the realm of line, proportion, and texture, not exposure everywhere.
